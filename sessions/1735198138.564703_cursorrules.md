# GiftGenius Documentation

## Overview
GiftGenius is an innovative app designed to simplify the gifting process for birthdays and other occasions. Using advanced AI and search capabilities, GiftGenius provides personalized gift suggestions based on the recipient's interests, hobbies, and social media activity. The app streamlines the entire gifting experience, from suggestion to purchase, while also generating affiliate revenue for the app owner.

## Features
- Personalized gift suggestions based on recipient information
- Integration with social media platforms for enhanced gift recommendations
- Budget management for individual recipients and overall yearly spending
- Automated purchasing through affiliate store partnerships
- Expandable affiliate network for increased revenue opportunities
- User-friendly interface for easy navigation and management

## Getting Started
### Adding Recipients
1. Navigate to the "Recipients" tab in the app.
2. Click on the "Add Recipient" button.
3. Fill in the following information:
   - Name
   - Date of birth
   - Hobbies and interests
   - Social media handles (optional)
   - Budget for the recipient
4. Click "Save" to add the recipient to your list.

### Setting Your Overall Budget
1. During the onboarding process, you will be prompted to set an overall yearly budget for gifting.
2. Allocate your budget across different categories of recipients, such as family, friends, and extended circles, using the provided pie chart.
3. Your budget allocations can be adjusted at any time in the "Settings" tab.

### Generating Gift Suggestions
1. Select a recipient from your list in the "Recipients" tab.
2. Click on the "Generate Gift Suggestions" button.
3. GiftGenius will analyze the recipient's information and provide a list of personalized gift suggestions.
4. Each suggestion will include a product description, price, and a link to purchase the item through an affiliate store.

### Purchasing Gifts
1. Review the generated gift suggestions for a recipient.
2. Select the desired gift by clicking on the "Add to Cart" button.
3. Proceed to checkout and complete the purchase using the app's secure payment system.
4. The purchased gift will be shipped directly to the recipient's address, if provided, or to your default shipping address.

## Affiliate Network
GiftGenius partners with a wide range of affiliate stores to provide diverse gift options and generate revenue for the app owner. The app's back office is designed to be future-proof, allowing for easy integration of new affiliate stores as the network expands.

### Adding New Affiliate Stores
1. App owner or designated AI agent identifies a potential new affiliate store.
2. The store's affiliate program is reviewed to ensure compatibility with GiftGenius.
3. The necessary API integrations and data connections are established between GiftGenius and the affiliate store.
4. The new affiliate store is added to the app's database, making its products available for gift suggestions.

## Frequently Asked Questions
1. **Q: Can I edit or delete a recipient's information after adding them?**
   A: Yes, you can edit or delete a recipient's information by navigating to the "Recipients" tab, selecting the recipient, and choosing the "Edit" or "Delete" option.

2. **Q: What happens if a suggested gift is out of stock or unavailable?**
   A: If a suggested gift is out of stock or unavailable, GiftGenius will automatically generate an alternative suggestion with similar characteristics to ensure you have a suitable gift option.

3. **Q: How does GiftGenius handle shipping and delivery of purchased gifts?**
   A: When you purchase a gift through GiftGenius, the affiliate store handles the shipping and delivery process. The gift will be shipped to the recipient's address, if provided, or to your default shipping address. Shipping times and costs may vary depending on the affiliate store's policies.

4. **Q: Can I request a refund for a purchased gift?**
   A: Refund policies may vary depending on the affiliate store from which the gift was purchased. Please contact the affiliate store directly for information on their refund process. If you require further assistance, our customer support team will be happy to help you navigate the refund request.

5. **Q: How does GiftGenius ensure the security of my payment information?**
   A: GiftGenius uses secure, encrypted payment processing systems to protect your sensitive payment information. We do not store any payment details on our servers, and all transactions are processed through trusted third-party payment providers.
