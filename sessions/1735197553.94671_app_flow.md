# App Flow Documentation

## Introduction
This document provides a comprehensive overview of the app flow for a gifting and birthday reminder application. The app aims to assist users who struggle with remembering birthdays and selecting appropriate gifts for their loved ones. By leveraging an LLM (Language Model) with search capabilities, the app provides personalized gift suggestions and simplifies the gifting process.

## User Onboarding
1. User creates an account by providing their email address and setting a password.
2. User logs in to the app using their credentials.

## Adding People and Birthdays
1. User navigates to the "People" section of the app.
2. User clicks on the "Add Person" button.
3. User enters the following information for each person:
   - Name
   - Birthday (date)
   - Relationship (e.g., nephew, friend, spouse)
   - Interests and hobbies
   - Budget range for gifts
4. User saves the person's information.
5. User can add multiple people to their list.

## Gift Suggestion Generation
1. The app uses the LLM with search capabilities to analyze the information provided about each person.
2. Based on the person's interests, hobbies, and budget range, the LLM generates personalized gift suggestions.
3. The app presents the gift suggestions to the user along with relevant details such as price, product description, and store information.

## Gift Approval and Purchase
1. User reviews the gift suggestions for each person.
2. User can select a suggested gift or request alternative suggestions by clicking on the "Think of Something Creative" button.
3. Once the user approves a gift suggestion, the app adds the selected item to the user's cart.
4. The app utilizes affiliate links to redirect the user to the respective online stores for purchase.
5. User completes the purchase process on the affiliated store's website.

## Affiliate Network Integration
1. The app maintains a growing network of affiliate partnerships with various online stores.
2. The back office of the app is designed to be future-proof, allowing easy integration of new affiliate stores.
3. An AI agent or the app administrator can continuously expand the affiliate network by connecting to new stores.
4. The app dynamically updates the gift suggestions based on the available affiliate stores and their product offerings.

## Birthday Reminders
1. The app sends automated birthday reminders to the user based on the stored birthdays of their loved ones.
2. Reminders are sent via email or push notifications a specified number of days before each birthday.
3. The user can customize the reminder settings, such as the number of days in advance and the preferred notification method.

## Revenue Model
1. The app generates revenue through affiliate commissions earned from user purchases made through the provided affiliate links.
2. When a user completes a purchase on an affiliated store's website, the app receives a percentage of the sale as a commission.

## Data Privacy and Security
1. The app ensures the privacy and security of user data by implementing industry-standard encryption and data protection measures.
2. User information, including personal details and gift preferences, is stored securely on the app's servers.
3. The app complies with relevant data protection regulations and provides users with control over their data.

## Future Enhancements
1. Integration with social media platforms to import birthdays and gather additional information about people.
2. Personalized gift wrapping and delivery options.
3. Collaborative gifting feature for group gifts.
4. Integration with virtual assistants for voice-based interactions.

## Conclusion
The gifting and birthday reminder app provides a convenient solution for users who struggle with remembering birthdays and selecting appropriate gifts. By leveraging an LLM with search capabilities and an extensive affiliate network, the app generates personalized gift suggestions and simplifies the gifting process. The app's revenue model is based on affiliate commissions, ensuring a sustainable business while providing value to its users.
