# Gift Suggestion and Affiliate Forwarding App

## 1. Overview
The Gift Suggestion and Affiliate Forwarding App is designed to help users who struggle with remembering birthdays and selecting appropriate gifts for their loved ones. The app utilizes a large language model (LLM) with search capabilities to provide personalized gift suggestions based on the information provided about each individual. The app also integrates with various affiliate stores, allowing users to automatically add the suggested gifts to their cart and earn affiliate commissions when purchases are made through the forwarded links.

## 2. Problem Statement
Many people find it challenging to remember birthdays and select suitable gifts for their friends and family members. This can lead to missed opportunities to show appreciation and maintain strong relationships. Additionally, the process of researching and purchasing gifts can be time-consuming and overwhelming for some individuals.

## 3. Solution
The Gift Suggestion and Affiliate Forwarding App addresses these challenges by providing a comprehensive solution that simplifies the gifting process. The app allows users to:
- Add people to their list along with their birthdays and relevant information
- Receive personalized gift suggestions based on the provided information
- Set a budget for each individual
- Approve suggested gifts with a single click
- Automatically add approved gifts to the respective store's cart using affiliate links
- Earn affiliate commissions when purchases are made through the forwarded links

## 4. Key Features
### 4.1 User Profile Management
- Users can create an account and manage their profile information
- Users can add, edit, and remove individuals from their gift list
- Each individual's profile includes their name, birthday, and relevant information for gift suggestions

### 4.2 Gift Suggestion Engine
- The app utilizes an LLM with search capabilities to generate personalized gift suggestions
- The LLM analyzes the information provided about each individual to suggest appropriate gifts
- Users can set a budget for each individual, and the app will provide suggestions within that budget

### 4.3 Affiliate Store Integration
- The app integrates with a wide range of affiliate stores to provide diverse gift options
- The back office system is designed to be future-proof, allowing for easy integration of new affiliate stores
- An AI agent or the app administrator can continuously expand the affiliate network to offer more recommendations

### 4.4 One-Click Approval and Forwarding
- Users can approve suggested gifts with a single click
- Approved gifts are automatically added to the respective store's cart using affiliate links
- Users are forwarded to the store's website to complete the purchase
- The app earns affiliate commissions when purchases are made through the forwarded links

### 4.5 Reminder System
- The app sends automated reminders to users about upcoming birthdays
- Reminders include gift suggestions and direct links to the app for easy access

## 5. Target Audience
The Gift Suggestion and Affiliate Forwarding App is designed for individuals who:
- Struggle with remembering birthdays and selecting appropriate gifts
- Want to show appreciation and maintain strong relationships with friends and family
- Prefer a simplified and streamlined gifting process
- Are interested in earning affiliate commissions while purchasing gifts

## 6. Monetization
The app generates revenue through affiliate commissions earned when users make purchases through the forwarded links. By partnering with a wide range of affiliate stores, the app can offer diverse gift options and maximize its earning potential.

## 7. Timeline
The development of the Gift Suggestion and Affiliate Forwarding App will be prioritized to launch as soon as possible. The exact timeline will depend on the complexity of the implementation, the availability of resources, and the speed of integrating with affiliate stores.
