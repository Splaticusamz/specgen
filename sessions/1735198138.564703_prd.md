# Product Requirements Document

## 1. Introduction
The Gifting Assistant is an app designed to help users who struggle with gifting and remembering birthdays. The app utilizes an AI language model with search capabilities to provide personalized gift suggestions based on the recipient's interests, hobbies, and other relevant information. The app also manages budgeting and automates the purchasing process through affiliate links, generating revenue for the app owner.

## 2. Problem Statement
Many people find it challenging to come up with creative gift ideas and remember important dates like birthdays, especially for extended family members or acquaintances. This often leads to last-minute purchases or generic gifts that may not align with the recipient's interests.

## 3. Solution
The Gifting Assistant app addresses this problem by:
- Allowing users to input relevant information about gift recipients, including their birthdays, hobbies, interests, and social media profiles
- Utilizing an AI language model to analyze the provided information and generate personalized gift suggestions
- Providing a budgeting system for each recipient and an overall yearly budget for the user
- Automating the purchasing process through affiliate links, streamlining the gifting experience for the user

## 4. Key Features

### 4.1 User Input
- Users can add gift recipients to the app, including their:
  - Name
  - Date of birth
  - Hobbies and interests
  - Social media profiles (for future social media scraping)
  - Budget for each recipient
- Users can set an overall yearly budget during onboarding, with a pie chart to allocate funds across different categories (e.g., family, friends, extended circles)

### 4.2 AI-Powered Gift Suggestions
- The app utilizes an AI language model with search capabilities to analyze the provided information and generate personalized gift suggestions for each recipient
- Gift suggestions are tailored to the recipient's interests, hobbies, and other relevant data points
- The app continuously learns and improves its suggestions based on user feedback and additional data

### 4.3 Budgeting and Purchasing
- The app manages the budget for each recipient, ensuring that suggestions align with the user's specified budget
- Users can approve suggested gifts, which are automatically added to a cart with affiliate links from respective stores
- The app handles the purchasing process, streamlining the experience for the user

### 4.4 Affiliate Network Integration
- The app generates revenue through affiliate partnerships with various stores
- The back office should be designed to be future-proof, allowing for easy integration of new affiliate stores to expand the network and improve gift suggestions

## 5. User Flow

1. User downloads and installs the Gifting Assistant app
2. User creates an account and sets up their profile, including an overall yearly budget
3. User adds gift recipients, providing relevant information such as name, date of birth, interests, and budget
4. The app analyzes the provided information and generates personalized gift suggestions for each recipient
5. User reviews and approves suggested gifts
6. Approved gifts are automatically added to a cart with affiliate links from respective stores
7. User completes the purchase through the app, which handles the transaction and delivery process
8. User provides feedback on the gift suggestions to improve future recommendations

## 6. Monetization
The Gifting Assistant app generates revenue through affiliate partnerships. When users purchase suggested gifts through the app's affiliate links, the app owner receives a commission from the respective stores. As the app's user base grows and more purchases are made through the affiliate network, the revenue potential increases.

## 7. Future Enhancements
- Integration with users' social media profiles to automatically scrape data about gift recipients, improving the accuracy and relevance of gift suggestions
- Expansion of the affiliate network to include a wider range of stores and products
- Incorporation of user feedback and purchase history to refine the AI language model's gift suggestion algorithms
- Collaboration with brands and retailers to offer exclusive discounts and promotions for app users
